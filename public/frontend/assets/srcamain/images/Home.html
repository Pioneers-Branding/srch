PROCESS

How It Works at Nutrihealth Genie?

01

Assessment of Nutritional Evaluation:

After you have signed up for the programme, You will receive a brief "Questionnaire" which will help us understand your physical condition including any medical ailments as well as your mental state of mind.
Our comprehensive team of nutritionists and therapists will go through the questionnaire and schedule an appointment based on your availability.

02

Personalized meal planning

The first appointment will be for an hour in which the nutritionist will understand your lifestyle, eating habits, sleeping pattern, walking regime, medical ailments, and most importantly your favourite food and defining your health goals.
Our expert nutritionist and therapist will curate a customised plan for you.
There will be 1 session scheduled with a therapist and a Doctor depending upon your requirement.
Post that every 4th day there will be an interaction to review eating & walking plan.

03

Community Support and Guidance:

Our experts provide comprehensive guidance on achieving balanced nutrition, emphasizing the importance of essential nutrients and practical strategies for incorporating them into your meals. You will be a part of "Nutrihealth Nourishers" where the members motivate and support each other to meet their health goals.know more

04

Consistent Support for Your Wellness Journey

Health & Happiness is a habit and that’s what we strive for. After you have achieved your health goals we will continue to support you through communities, wellness programs and more.
Personalized wellness check-ins to ensure long-term success and adaptation to your evolving needs.

Get your answer here

Below you’ll find answers to some of the most frequently asked question.
We are constantly adding most asked question to this page.

1. How do I get started with Nutrihealth Genie, Health and Wellness Solutions' programs?

Starting with our Health and Wellness Solutions is simple. You can visit our website to explore the various programs we offer, select the one that best suits your needs, and complete the sign-up process online. If you need assistance, our team is available to guide you through the process and help you choose the right program for your health and wellness goals.

2. How will the consultation with Nutritionist/Therapist/ Doctor be conducted?

We understand that your health and convenience are our top priorities. Our consultations are designed to be accessible to all clients, regardless of where they live. While in-person meetings are available in Delhi and Rohtak, Online Consultations are done via phone, Zoom, What’s App, or Google Meet.Video meets are convenient and accessible from everywhere.

3. How do I maintain my weight after the weight loss program?

To maintain your weight after the program, consistency is key. NhG offers a Weight Maintenance plan that includes healthy eating habits, regular exercise, and ongoing community support to keep you motivated. We also educate you on the nutritional value of foods, helping you make informed choices that support long-term weight maintenance and overall health.

4. How will my progress be reviewed in Nutrihealth Genie?

Nutrihealthgenie is a Nutrition and Wellness platform designed to help you achieve your Health goals, whether it’s weight loss, muscle gain, or Lifestyle disorders like diabetes. With holistic guidance, real-time support through WhatsApp via your personal Health Buddy, and timely reminders, we’ll be there to support you every step of the way.

After receiving your blood reports, we’ll deliver your customized meal plan and start-up kit directly to your personal email or WhatsApp. Additionally, free supplements will be sent to the address you provided.

Following this, we’ll schedule a one-on-one call to further tailor your plan. The time of the call can be discussed and adjusted as per your schedule.

You’ll also be welcomed into the Nutrihealth Nourishers Community, where you’ll receive motivational tips and customized recipes to keep you on track and ensure long-term success.

We’ll provide guidance on dining out so you can stick to your goals, and by tracking your daily diet and water intake, the NhG team will help you make informed food choices and understand the nutritional value of what you eat, making it a sustainable, lifelong plan.

5. Will the Online Results be as good as coming to the Clinic?

Yes, the online results will be just as effective as coming to the clinic. Our comprehensive online programs are designed to deliver the same high-quality guidance, support, and personalized care. With real-time consultations, tailored meal plans, and continuous monitoring, you'll receive the same level of attention and results as you would in person.

6. Is weight loss guaranteed?

While we provide you with all the tools, guidance, and support needed for successful weight loss, individual results can vary. Success largely depends on your diligence in following the program, including sticking to meal plans, staying active, and engaging with our support systems. It's important to note that medical issues or genetic factors may sometimes affect your ability to lose weight. In such cases, we will work closely with your medical team to do our best to make the program effective for you.

7. Can I start with 3 months plan and then extend it to 6 or 12 months?

No extensions will be possible once you have made the payment, you will have to re-register.

8. How should I know which duration of the Health & Wellness Solution to be taken?

The best package duration depends on your health goals and how much support you need. Our team can help assess your situation and recommend the most suitable option for you, ensuring you get the right level of guidance to achieve your desired results.
